Enterprise Desktop Engineer

In this course we will cover a variety of topics. We will discuss cloud concepts, security, endpoint management, virtual desktops with Azure and VMWare and other technical requirements to prepare you to become an enterprise desktop engineer. In addition to the topics mentioned, you will be required to take a weekly written test, conduct a weekly one on one interview and complete a weekly quality audit. Quality audits are technical topics that you have learned that can be presented in power point and demo'd


Topics that we will cover throughout this course, listed below:

  • Azure Essentials
  • Administration and Scripting
  • Networking and Databases
  • Identity and Access Management
  • Endpoint Management and Security
